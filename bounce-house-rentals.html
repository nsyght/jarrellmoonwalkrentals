Bounce House Rental FAQ

Everything Jarrell TX families ask about renting a moonwalk or bounce house.

Most themed bounce houses rent for $155 in Jarrell TX. Holiday-themed units like 4th of July start at $205. Specialty units like Easter are $265 and the Animal Kingdom is $355. All pricing includes delivery, setup, and pickup with no hidden fees.
Bounce house and moonwalk are two names for the same rental. In Texas, inflatable jump houses are commonly called moonwalks. They are also known as jumpers, jump houses, bouncy castles, and spacewalks. All refer to the same inflatable structure delivered to your Jarrell TX address.
A standard 15x15ft bounce house safely holds 6 to 8 children at a time depending on age and weight. Younger children under 5 should have their own jump time separate from older children to avoid collisions. Adult supervision is required at all times.
Yes. We deliver bounce houses to all Jarrell TX neighborhoods including Double Creek Estates, properties near Jarrell ISD school campuses, and all addresses in ZIP 76537. Call (737) 234-3992 to confirm delivery to your specific address.
A standard bounce house requires a flat area of roughly 18x18 feet with at least 15 feet of overhead clearance. You will also need a 110v outdoor electrical outlet within 50 feet and a clear path from the street for our crew. We confirm your exact dimensions during booking.
