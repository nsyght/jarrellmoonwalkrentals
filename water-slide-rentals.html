Setup Requirements

What You Need Before We Arrive

Water slides are straightforward to set up. Here is everything your Jarrell TX location needs before our crew arrives with your rental.

💧

Garden Hose Access

A standard outdoor hose within 50ft of the setup area. We connect directly -- no special plumbing needed.

📐

Flat Open Space

Flat grassy or paved area with no overhead obstructions. Exact dimensions confirmed during booking.

⚡

110v Outdoor Outlet

A standard outdoor electrical outlet within 50ft to power the blower that keeps your water slide inflated.

🌿

Clear Access Path

A clear 4ft-wide path from the street to the setup area for our crew to carry equipment to your yard.
